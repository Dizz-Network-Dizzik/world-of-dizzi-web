Journey

Dated, not narrated

Every node below is a document in the public repository, carrying the date in its own header or its own filename. Nothing here is reconstructed from memory.

Milestones

  1. 8 June 2026

    The trading foundations

    The oldest dated documents in the repository: a system overview of the trading AI, fundamental research, and a system-check review. The network began at its riskiest corner, on purpose — if the rules hold where money is, they hold everywhere.

  2. 9–10 June 2026

    Research tooling and the first system checks

    A metrics study, a research tool work package, an improvement backlog, and the migration into a single folder. The habit of writing a check down before running it starts here.

  3. 10–11 June 2026

    Vision, laws, master plan

    Vision and requirements, the system overview, the design system, the master plan and the per-app fundamentals — plus the first research waves for the network itself. Four days of writing before the architecture existed.

  4. 12 June 2026

    The app contract, identity and the security concept

    The single most consequential day. The app contract fixed what every application must answer; the identity service fixed how tiers and tokens work; the security concept named the threat model and the honest residual risks. Everything after this is an application of these three.

  5. 13–17 June 2026

    The validation nights

    Three dated overnight training runs on the trading side. The working rules plan for these explicitly: a big package is written up, the plan is read back once, the human confirms, then the machine works through the night alone.

  6. 15–19 June 2026

    Cross-connections and the monorepo

    The hand-over contracts between applications, the MCP gateway that pulls every tool to one point, and the consolidation into one repository. The health watch came back green after the cutover, reboot test included.

  7. 20–27 June 2026

    Connectivity, the area model, deep audits

    Connectivity as a core goal, the meta connector, the unification of the UI kit, the area-type model, the core's continuous loop and the future architecture roadmap — bracketed by two deep audits on 25 June.

  8. 3–4 July 2026

    Agents, exports, tax, trading validation

    A dense two days of contracts: the swappable local model runtime, the autonomy ladder T0–T3 with an emergency stop, read-only bank sync, the tax transport path, the area canon — and the app export contract, which specifies a single application as a self-contained, sellable bundle.

  9. 11–20 July 2026

    Chronicle, constitution, interaction physics

    A chronicle kernel with its own audit core; the project's essence pinned at constitutional rank in the laws, unchangeable by any session or refactor; and version 4.5 of the spin-physics specification.

  10. 23 July 2026

    The curated public snapshot

    966 files, published as one commit. Source-visible, not open source. Some modules deliberately left out. This website is the second step of the same move: the repository proves it, the site explains it.
